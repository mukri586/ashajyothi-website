No marketing budget. No middlemen.

AshaJyothi is run by the same family that started it. Our overhead stays under 10%. Every donation is acknowledged with a real human reply.

80G

Tax-deductible in India.

All non-cash donations are exempt under Sections 80G & 12A of the Income Tax Act, 1961. We send a digital 80G receipt within 48 hours.
