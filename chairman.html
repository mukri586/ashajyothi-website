Chief Functionary · Secretary · Correspondent · Since 1998

Founder of AshaJyothi since 1998. Started with two abandoned children and a borrowed room — built a permanent campus of 70+ children with severe disabilities. Twenty-seven years of unbroken care, still on campus every day. Her work spans medical rehabilitation, inclusive education, women's empowerment, disaster relief, and judicial child welfare across Andhra Pradesh.

What she built/ 02

27 years of work.

Four areas — one sustained commitment.

Building from nothing — permanently.

Scaled AshaJyothi from 2 children in a borrowed room to a fully self-sufficient campus of 100+ residents. Every brick was fought for.

🌱
Founded 1998Started with 2 abandoned children at Hanuman Junction — a family's grief turned outward.
🏗️
Permanent CampusSecured land, construction, and multi-facility infrastructure with international partner SDWH, Holland.
⚡
Self-SufficiencySolar power, borewell systems, hydrotherapy rooms — campus runs independently, 365 days a year.
⚖️
CWC Chairperson · 2015–2019Appointed to function as a Bench of First Class Judicial Magistrates, West Godavari District.
